Tenets of Agile

Individuals and interactions over processes and tools We value people and their contributions, and face-to-face conversations. Working software over comprehensive documentationfocus is on incrementally delivering working software rather than comprehensive design which may change or not be used.

collaboration over contract negotiation involving customers early in the process helps create more accurate prioritized deliverables.

to change over following a plan ability to react to changing customer or business priorities (within reason), or industry standards helps create more value for the customer.
